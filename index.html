Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know.

Is FinWave really free?

Yes! The Free plan includes unlimited transactions, 3 budgets, and 2 goals. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited everything.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, biometric auth, and never store sensitive data. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I link multiple bank accounts?

Yes! FinWave supports linking multiple accounts, credit cards, and wallets. Manage everything from one dashboard.

How do I export my data?

Go to Transactions → Export to download a CSV. You can also export all data from Settings → Data & Export.
